About us

Users can create accounts or log in to track their favorite products, reviews, and preferences. Users can input product details, such as the product name, brand, category, and description.The website can utilize geolocation services (e.g., GPS) to determine the user's current location or allow them to manually input their location. A database of local shops and stores is maintained, including their contact details, operating hours, and product offerings.After the user enters product details, the website displays a list of nearby shops where the product is available. The results show shop names, distances, product prices, and user reviews. Users can directly contact the shop or place an order if the shop offers delivery services.This concept not only benefits users by simplifying their shopping experience but also supports local businesses by increasing their visibility and customer reach.
